Create a program that accepts a user's name and age as input and prints out a welcome message to the user, referencing their name and age. If the user's name is 'John' and their age is '22', the program should print 'Hello, I am John! I am 22 years old.'


Sample Input:
John
22

Sample Output:
Hello, I am John! I am 22 years old.


Sample Input:
Alice
30

Sample Output:
Hello, I am Alice! I am 30 years old.



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 5 seconds
